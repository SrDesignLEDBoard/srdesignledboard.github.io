Vision

Sports Scoreboard aims to keep a sports fan up to date with ongoing game scores, visually alerting the user when games begin, end and when points are made.

Mission

Our primary mission is to deliver a product that will fulfill the needs of sports fans that want to be able to see live scores at home. It will serve to visually alert fans about past, live, and oncoming games. The user will also be able to personally customize the Sports Scoreboard using several user-specific capabilities as to what leagues, teams, and matches that it will display.
