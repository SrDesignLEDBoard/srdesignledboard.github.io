Scoreboard - CSE 4316 Project by team Mavericks

Vision

Sports Scoreboard aims to keep a sports fan up to date with ongoing game scores, visually alerting theuser when games begin, end and when points are made.

Mission

Our primary mission is to deliver a product that will fulfill the needs of sports fans that want to be ableto see live scores at home. It will also serve to visually alert fans about past, live, and oncoming games.They will also be able to personally customize the Sports Scoreboard and use it to decorate their space.

Our aimed features

  • Support for top four leagues NBA, MLB, NHL, NFL.
  • Functionality to switch between matches or teams using buttons on the system.
  • Ability to favorite teams to better follow them.
  • Visual alerts for game updates and upcoming games.
